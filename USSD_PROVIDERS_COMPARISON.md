### Option 4: Register a Company 🏢

**In Mozambique:**

**Types:**
1. **Sole Proprietorship (Empresário em Nome Individual)**
   - Simplest form
   - Individual ownership
   - Lower costs

2. **Limited Company (Sociedade Unipessoal por Quotas)**
   - Single-person company
   - Limited liability
   - More professional

**Process:**
1. Choose business name
2. Register with IPAJ (Instituto dos Registos e Notariado)
3. Get NUIT (Tax ID)
4. Register with tax authority
5. Open business bank account

**Cost:** ~$100-500 USD
**Time:** 1-4 weeks
**Required:** ID, address proof, business plan

**Resources:**
- IPAJ: https://www.ipaj.gov.mz/
- GAZEDA (for investment): https://www.gazeda.gov.mz/
